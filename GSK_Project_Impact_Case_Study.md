# GSK Project Impact Case Study

## 📌 Overview
Worked as a Project Manager supporting cross-functional teams across operations, marketing, and product functions within a global pharmaceutical environment.

---

## 🎯 Problem
Project workflows and reporting processes lacked standardisation, leading to delays in decision-making and limited visibility for stakeholders.

---

## 🛠 Actions Taken
- Developed KPI dashboards using Excel and Power BI to track project performance  
- Standardised reporting templates across multiple teams  
- Coordinated with 5+ stakeholders to align deliverables and timelines  
- Identified workflow inefficiencies and implemented structured tracking mechanisms  
- Automated reporting processes to reduce manual effort  

---

## 📊 Results
- Improved delivery efficiency by approximately **15%**  
- Reduced manual reporting effort by **20% (around 5 hours per week)**  
- Enhanced visibility for stakeholders through structured dashboards  
- Enabled faster and more informed decision-making  

---

## 💡 Key Skills Demonstrated
- Project Planning & Tracking  
- Stakeholder Management  
- KPI & Performance Analysis  
- Process Optimisation  
- Reporting & Governance
